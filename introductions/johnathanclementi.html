My Interests and Experiences

I studied environmental science and computer science at Marist College in Poughkeepsie, New York and graduated in 2018. Since then, I have worked for the planning and assessment officies in county government maintaining the parcel datasets and producing maps for various projects. I have also volunteered with the URISA GISCorps Disaster PhotoMappers, in which we collect images and videos from social media in the immediate hours after a natural disaster and geolocate those images to provide agencies such as FEMA get a better idea of the damages.
