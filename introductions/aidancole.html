My Interests and Experiences

I am straight out of undergraduate from Cornell University, where I obtained a B.S. in Environment and Sustainability and minors in Music, Business, and Climate Change Studies. This one class led me here, to the University of Pennsylvania, to pursue a Master's in Urban Spatial Analytics and to, ultimately, help cities become more climate- adaptive.

In this course, I hope to learn why JavaScript is the most commonly used coding language in the world. What can it do that Python or R can't? More specifically, I want to learn more about different ways to visualize data, as well as how to build web-based applications.
